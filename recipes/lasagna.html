Lasagna

Delicious ground beef lasagna with bechamel and tomato sauce.

  • Lasagna Layers
  • Tomato Pure
  • Onions
  • Green Pepper
  • Ground Beef
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • White Flour
  • Oil
  1. Heat a frying man to medium/high heat and add the onions and green peppers.
  2. Onces the vegetables are Cooked add the ground beef and cook all together.
  3. After 5 minutes, af the tomato pure and let it reduce.
  4. On another pan, add 2 spoons of butter and let it melt. Onces hot, add the flour and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Ad a glass milk to the pan with the flour and butter and mix it well with a wooden spoon. Let it thicken, and the lasagna will be ready.
  6. Cook the lasagna layer as specified by the brand.
  7. In a container for the oven, add the first lasagna layers, and alternate the bechamel and ground beef filling.
  8. Put it in the oven at 200C for 30min.
  9. Wait for it to cool down and enjoy.
